Here is just a glimpse of what we do!

PODCAST: We produce podcasts to inform listeners about different aspects of mental health!

OUT OF THE DARKNESS WALK: With the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention, UNC held an Out of the Darkness Walk to send the message that no one is alone.

TEAM NIGHTS: Each month, we host a team night for members and students to get together and have meaningful conversations.

DEAR STRANGER INITIATIVE: Immediately following the tragedies that occurred on campus, we decided that kindness needed to be spread on campus. We hand wrote 500+ letters and distributed them to strangers.

ALL OF US RESEARCH: With the national group for Active Minds, we discussed mental wellness, personalized medicine, and how they intersect. The All of Us research program is through the National Institutes of Health.

APPRECIATION FOR NURSES: We wanted to give a BIG thank you to our nursing students and to nurses everywhere! We have seen how hard the pandemic continues to be on them. We gave nurses letters, candy, and resources at our table!
